Delete Your EvenGo Account

Last updated: 2026-05-03

Delete From Inside The App

  1. Open EvenGo.
  2. Open the account menu in the dashboard header.
  3. Choose Delete account.
  4. Confirm the deletion dialog.

Deleting your account is permanent. Your account, your guest session if applicable, events you created, and your event participations are deleted.

Request Deletion Without The App

If you no longer have access to the app, send an account deletion request to the developer contact email shown on the EvenGo Google Play listing. Include the email address or username used for your EvenGo account and the subject "EvenGo account deletion request".

What Gets Deleted

  • Your user account and authentication data.
  • Events you created and planning details stored for those events.
  • Your participant records for events you joined.
  • Your guest identifier if you used guest mode.
